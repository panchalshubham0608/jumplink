JumpLink Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 21, 2024

1. Information We Collect

1.1. URL Mappings:

What we collect: JumpLink allows you to create custom URL mappings. These mappings are stored locally on your device and, if you’re signed in to Chrome, are synced across your devices using Chrome’s chrome.storage.sync.

How we collect it: The URL mappings are created and managed directly by you through the extension’s interface.

2. How We Use Information

2.1. URL Redirection:

JumpLink uses the URL mappings you create to redirect you to the corresponding long URLs when you use the short URL in the Chrome omnibox or as a keyword.

2.2. Data Storage and Sync:

The mappings you create are stored locally in your Chrome browser and are synced across devices if Chrome sync is enabled. This allows you to access your mappings from any device where you are signed in to Chrome.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your data: JumpLink does not transmit your URL mappings or any other data to external servers or third parties. All data remains within your browser and synced devices.

4. Permissions

4.1. Storage Permission:

Why we need it: The storage permission allows JumpLink to save your custom URL mappings and sync them across your devices. This ensures that your shortcuts are available wherever you use Chrome.

5. Your Choices and Rights

5.1. Managing Your Data:

You can view, edit, or delete your URL mappings at any time through the extension’s options page.

5.2. Removing the Extension:

If you decide you no longer wish to use JumpLink, you can uninstall the extension from Chrome, which will also remove all data associated with it from your device.

6. Security

We take security seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect your data within the scope of the Chrome extension. However, please note that the security of your data also depends on you keeping your Chrome account secure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
